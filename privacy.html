Overview

"PaiTa" is a photography assistant app. We take your privacy very seriously. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data.

Specifically:

  • We do not collect your name, email, or any contact information
  • We do not collect your location data
  • We do not track your usage behavior
  • We do not use any third-party analytics tools

Camera and Photo Library Permissions

This app requires the following permissions to function:

  • Camera access: To take photos

Your photos are stored only on your device. We cannot access them and do not upload them to any server.

Template photos saved in the app are stored locally on your device only. We have no servers and do not store any of your data.

This app is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
